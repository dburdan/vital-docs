## Connecting Garmin to Vital

Vital! You can do this with Vital Link Widget or programatically like any other [OAuth](/api-reference/link/link-oauth-provider) providers.

## Vital Widget

To connect your device with the _widget_, follow the link wizard ensuring and enter your username and password.

<Warning>
  This provider doesn't send historical webhooks. This is in constrast with
  other providers we have. This means you will receive `daily.data.<resource_type>.created`.
</Warning>

## Webhooks

On successful connection, you will receive:

- [Connection create](/webhooks/data_flow#connection-created) wehbook.
- [Daily webhooks](/webhooks/data_flow#daily-data-flow) `daily.data.<resource_type>.created` and `daily.data.<resource_type>.updated` are issued, as new or more up-to-date data becomes available.
