## Connecting My Fitness Pal to Vital

You can now link My Fitness Pal accounts to Vital! You can do this with Vital Link Widget or programatically like any other [password](/api-reference/link/link-email-provider).

## Vital Widget

To connect your device with the *widget*, follow the link wizard ensuring and enter your username and password.

## Webhooks

On successful connection, you will recieve:

- [Connection create](/webhooks/data_flow#connection-created) wehbook.
- [Historical Meals](/webhooks/data_flow#historical-data-flow) webhook, implying that the last *2 weeks* of data has been fetched successfully.
- [Daily webhooks](/webhooks/data_flow#daily-data-flow) `data.daily.meal.created` and `data.daily.meal.updated` will also be issued to your team as new or more up-to-date data becomes available during the day.
